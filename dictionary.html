WordMeaning
Pop Pop is the feeling of a snappy new skateboard. You can understand the skateboard term pop if you skate. It is more a feel than anything else.
Backside Backside – tricks that cause your back side to face the direction of travel or obstacle.
Frontside Frontside – tricks that cause your front side to face the direction of travel or obstacle.
Mongo A style of pushing where the back foot is kept on the board and pushing is done with the front foot.
Goofy Riding with the right foot forward, the opposite of "Regular".
Regular Riding with the left foor forward, the opposite of "Goofy".
Nollie Riding with right foot forward, just like Goofy but with skateboard backwards, opposite of "Fakie".
Fakie Riding with left foot forward, just like Regular but with skateboard backwards, opposite of "Nollie".
Nose Front of your skateboard.
Tail Back of your skateboard.
Grind Scraping axles on any surface like a curb or railing.
