Before You Start

I'm sure that probably you know it already, but in order to perform a trick, you need to train it for hours or days, it won't land on the first time. Also when you're struggling with Ollie don't even try to get to a Kickflip.

Choose a Trick

In brief:

1. Place your feet
2. Crouch at the knees
3. Jump fast
4. Pop hard at the same time
5. Rise with the board
6. Level it out a bit
7. Start coming back down
8. Land on the board and crouch for balance


If you need practice, you should practice two moves. Jump, and Pop.
The Jump
While rolling crouch down at the knees. Using your crouched knees, jump up and land back on your board. Crouch for balance
The Pop
While standing still on the skateboard, pop the tail by hitting it down on the ground hard. Stay on the board
